Loan Telecaller Enquiry Form

Telecaller can fill details manually or upload customer documents like Aadhaar, PAN, bank statement, or payslip. The system reads text from supported files and auto-fills matching fields wherever possible.

0. Document Auto-Fill

Upload one or more supporting documents. Supported best: text PDFs and TXT files. For image-only PDFs or images, browser-only extraction is limited and backend OCR is recommended.

Suggested usage:
  • Telecaller fills all details directly during call, or
  • Telecaller asks customer for Aadhaar, PAN, payslip, bank statement, then clicks auto-fill.
  • After auto-fill, telecaller reviews missing fields and completes the rest manually.
